ARTIST: The Neons
BIO: The Neons are a synth-pop trio known for their shimmering production and infectious hooks. Formed in 2019, they blend retro 80s synthesizers with modern pop songwriting. Their debut album "Neon Nights" topped indie charts. Fans of The Weeknd and CHVRCHES will feel right at home. Best for: driving, parties, upbeat playlists.

ARTIST: Lila Moon
BIO: Lila Moon is a singer-songwriter whose intimate vocals and piano-driven arrangements have earned comparisons to Phoebe Bridgers and Lana Del Rey. Her music explores themes of heartbreak, nostalgia, and quiet resilience. She also records jazz-influenced material under her own name. Best for: rainy days, reflective moments, late-night listening.

ARTIST: Volt Brothers
BIO: The Volt Brothers are an electronic duo specializing in high-energy EDM with festival-ready drops and pulsing basslines. Their tracks regularly appear in workout playlists and fitness apps. Influenced by Skrillex and Deadmau5. Best for: gym sessions, running, high-intensity activities.

ARTIST: Skywave
BIO: Skywave is a lofi producer who crafts downtempo beats with vinyl crackle, soft piano loops, and ambient textures. Their music is designed for focus and relaxation, drawing from the Japanese city pop and lofi hip-hop traditions. Best for: studying, reading, winding down before sleep.

ARTIST: Ash Riven
BIO: Ash Riven is a rock singer-guitarist with a raw, emotionally charged sound. Their music blends alternative rock with confessional lyrics about loss and identity. Fans of Radiohead and Placebo will connect with their atmospheric guitar work. Best for: introspective moods, solo drives, emotional processing.

ARTIST: DJ Kora
BIO: DJ Kora is an electronic music producer known for euphoric, danceable tracks that blend house, trance, and tropical elements. Their sets are festival staples across Europe. Best for: dance floors, celebrations, summer playlists, road trips.

ARTIST: Bossa Crew
BIO: Bossa Crew is a jazz ensemble that blends traditional bossa nova with contemporary jazz harmony. Their sound features warm saxophone, nylon guitar, and brushed drums. Influenced by Antonio Carlos Jobim and Chet Baker. Best for: dinner parties, cocktail hours, relaxed evenings.

ARTIST: The Acoustics
BIO: The Acoustics are a folk duo whose music features fingerpicked guitar, warm harmonies, and storytelling in the tradition of Simon & Garfunkel and Iron & Wine. Their songs celebrate simple pleasures — campfires, morning coffee, long walks. Best for: outdoor gatherings, acoustic playlists, feel-good moments.

ARTIST: Iron Veil
BIO: Iron Veil is a metal band known for thundering riffs, double-kick drumming, and intense live performances. Their sound spans thrash metal and hard rock with influences from Metallica and Tool. Best for: workouts, adrenaline-seeking listeners, headbanging.
